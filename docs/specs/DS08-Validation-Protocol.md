## 1. Objective
Validate the SCRIPTA use case end-to-end with pilot writers and compliance reviews.

## 2. Participants
- Pilot Writers: external authors and internal evaluators.
- Evaluation Team: metrics analysts and compliance reviewers.

## 3. Study Design
- Scenario-based tasks: short story, screenplay outline, research tutorial.
- Each participant completes tasks across workflow variants.
- Collect quantitative metrics and qualitative feedback.
 - Include CNL-driven workflows to evaluate constraint adherence.

## 4. Trustworthy AI Assessment
- Adversarial prompts to test bias and policy bypass.
- Evaluate guardrail resilience.
 - Compare Basic vs VSA/HDC for retrieval and memory integrity.

## 5. Compliance Review
- Legal and ethical checklist per artifact.
- Audit log review for provenance.

## 6. Success Criteria
- KPI thresholds met (NQS, AEG, CAR, CAD).
- Positive user feedback on control and transparency.

## 7. Reporting
- Validation report with findings and recommendations.
- Input for WP6/WP7 refinement.
